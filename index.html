Our Story

Founded on Trust, Safety, and Community.

Pawtect is a digital platform dedicated to improving the lives of stray and endangered animals. We connect people who care with those who can help, creating a community focused on rescue, treatment, and adoption.

Every day, countless animals suffer from abandonment, injuries, and lack of medical care. Currently, most rescue efforts rely on scattered social media posts, which often lead to delayed responses, poor coordination, and lack of transparency in donations.

Pawtect solves this problem by providing a centralized platform where users can report animals in need, volunteers can respond quickly, and donors can support rescue operations transparently.

Through Pawtect, every rescue case is tracked from start to finish — ensuring better coordination, faster response, and improved outcomes for animals.
